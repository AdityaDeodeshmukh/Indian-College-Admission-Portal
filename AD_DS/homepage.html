About

Indian College Admission Portal (ICAP) simplifies and streamlines the undergraduate College Admission process. The main motive behind the creation of this Portal is to make the admission process more efficient and to thereby reduce the burden among students by putting everything in a single page. We have a recommendation system where the student would have to answer a few simple questions and he/she would be recommended a career path. In addition to this, we also have a tracker where the student can track his/her progress for a particular university
